Interview Mini-Project: 10-K Report Data
Extraction and Evaluation

Objective
Build a system that extracts specific numerical data from 10-K reports of public companies and
evaluates the accuracy of those extractions.

Requirements

Input
Obtain at least ten 10-K reports from different public companies. All reports must be provided in PDF
format.
Output
Data Extraction
Select three distinct numerical fields to extract from each report. Aim for fields that are reasonably
challenging — not so simple that extraction is trivial (e.g., a single top-level "Total Revenue" line), but not
so obscure that they become a research rabbit hole. Good candidates include segment revenues,
geographic breakdowns, or operating metrics that require some interpretation to locate consistently
across reports. The goal is to have a meaningful but completable iteration story: your first pass should
have some errors worth analyzing and fixing.
Evaluation
Prepare a ground truth dataset for your chosen fields and evaluate your extraction results against it
using appropriate metrics.

Iteration and Improvement
Initial Challenge: The selected fields should present a meaningful challenge — your first extraction
pass should not be perfect.
Refinement Cycle: Perform at least one iteration to improve extraction quality based on observed
errors.
Error Analysis: Be prepared to walk through your error analysis methodology and explain how it
informed your improvements.

Project Guidelines
Individual Work: Complete this project independently.
AI Assistance: You are encouraged to use AI tools for research and coding support.

Bonus

Build a data visualization dashboard to display extracted values and system performance. Suggested
visualizations:
Compare the three extracted fields (e.g., revenue, assets) across all ten reports.
Show how extraction accuracy metrics changed across refinement cycles.

Presentation

Present your project, covering your approach and findings. Be prepared to make live modifications to
your system during the interview to demonstrate a thorough understanding of the code and its underlying
principles.
